I am fascinated to how geometry can recreate our world in the virtual environment and to how humans interact with this boundless reconstructed land. For instance, we can model an organoid in the computer and 3D print it; we can look at cells in virtual reality and feel like we have shrunk; and we can X-ray CT a car motor to see the inside without even slicing it.
